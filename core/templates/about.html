The Fableseed Gardener's Manual

A step-by-step guide to planting and growing stories

  • Step 1
    Create your gardener identity.
    Visit your Garden, choose a display name and a profile picture, and write a small bio about yourself.
  • Step 2
    Sow seeds in the Nursery.
    Visit the Nursery and plant a Fableseed of just a couple of lines with a title. Choose the type of flower your prompt will grow into - each blossom symbolises the spirit of your story.
  • Step 3
    Earn Fablebuds.
    Every planted Fableseed grants precious Fablebuds - these let you grow other people's stories.
  • Step 4
    Browse and Grow.
    Browse the Nursery to discover story seeds planted by other gardeners - choose a flower to add to your profile garden.
  • Step 5
    Spends Fablebuds to grow Fablebranches.
    Every Fablebranch you write blooms that flower in your profile garden. Grow more branches to watch your flowers multiply.
  • Step 6
    Propagate your plants.
    Every Fablebranch you write blooms that flower in your profile garden. Grow more branches to watch your flowers multiply.
  • Step 7
    Cultivate your garden of you.
    Your user profile becomes a beautiful garden - and a living record of the stories you've helped grow. Clicking on a flower leads to the original Fableseed that granted you that blossom, and so your garden becomes a living archive of stories.
