About Fableseed

Welcome to Fableseed, a collaborative storytelling platform, where stories grow like plants and words breathe life into a blooming world.

In Fableseed, stories are as essential as oxygen. Plant the seeds of imagination and watch your own personal garden blossom with vibrant flowers, each one nurtured by the tales you and others tell.

These blossoms aren't just beautiful - they cradle your stories in their petals, keeping them alive in the last garden at the end of the world

The Fableseed Gardener's Manual

A step-by-step guide to planting and growing stories

  • Create your gardener identity. Visit your Garden and choose a display name and a profile picture, and write a small bio about yourself.
  • Sow seeds in the Nursery. Visit the Nursery and plant a Fableseed of just a couple of lines with a title. Choose the type of flower your prompt will grow into - each blossom symbolises the spirit of your story.
  • Earn Fablebuds. Every planted Fableseed grants precious Fablebuds - these let you grow other people's stories.
  • Browse and Grow. Browse the Nursery to discover story seeds planted by other gardeners - choose a flower to add to your profile garden.
  • Spends Fablebuds to grow Fablebranches. Spend a Fablebud to add a branch to someone else's story. This ensures every story is nurtured with intention and gives purpose to planting your seeds.
  • Propagate your plants. Every Fablebranch you write blooms that flower in your profile garden. Grow more branches to watch your flowers multiply.
  • Cultivate your garden of you. Your user profile becomes a beautiful garden - and a living record of the stories you've helped grow. Clicking on a flower leads to the original Fableseed that granted you that blossom, and so your garden becomes a living archive of stories.

As stories grow, so does the world

Together, we are planting something that will outlast the end.
